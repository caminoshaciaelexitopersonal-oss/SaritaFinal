# Strategic Purpose Audit - SARITA Phase 94

## Component Analysis

### MetaLearningEngine
- **Status**: Operational (Phase 93).
- **Findings**: Optimizes governance based on past effectiveness, but lacks a "Desired Future State" to guide this optimization.
- **Gaps**: Learning is reactive, not proactive. No teleological guidance.

### ConstitutionalIntelligenceCore
- **Status**: Operational (Phase 93).
- **Findings**: Stores meta-knowledge and guidance.
- **Gaps**: Does not distinguish between operational guidance and long-term strategic objectives.

### GovernanceStrategyOptimizer
- **Status**: Operational (Phase 93).
- **Findings**: Adjusts risk tolerance and complexity.
- **Gaps**: Optimization is purely mathematical (minimizing failure), not strategic (achieving a specific evolutionary goal).

### AdaptiveMaturityCalculator
- **Status**: Operational (Phase 93).
- **Findings**: Measures maturity based on accuracy and success.
- **Gaps**: Does not account for "Alignment with Purpose" as a maturity factor.

## Detected Gaps
1. **Absence of Explicit Goals**: The system knows "how" to improve, but not "what for".
2. **Short-term Optimization**: All metrics are based on recent history (100 cycles), with no 1000-cycle projection.
3. **Teleological Drift**: Without a sovereign purpose engine, SARITA could optimize itself into a state that is efficient but contradictory to its founding principles.
