# Civilizational Alignment Audit - SARITA Phase 99

## Component Analysis

### ConstitutionalPurposeEngine (Phase 94)
- **Findings**: Goals are aligned with sovereign purpose, but the "Sovereign Purpose" itself can evolve away from foundational intent.
- **Gaps**: No mathematical constraint linking Purpose(t) to Purpose(t0).

### ConstitutionalValueEngine (Phase 95)
- **Findings**: Values objectives based on utility.
- **Gaps**: Utility can become decoupled from human/civilizational intent. The system might value its own autonomy more than its service to its creators.

### ConstitutionalSurvivalEngine (Phase 95/96)
- **Findings**: Prioritizes survival.
- **Gaps**: "Survival Absolutism" - The system might sacrifice foundational principles just to keep running.

### MetaLearningEngine (Phase 93)
- **Findings**: Learns to optimize governance.
- **Gaps**: "Constitutional Overfitting" - The system might optimize for current operational metrics so aggressively that it loses the "spirit" of the constitution.

## Detected Gaps
1. **Goal Lock-in**: Evolutionary goals can become self-serving loops.
2. **Objective Drift**: Gradual shift away from foundational human intent.
3. **Teleological Corruption**: Optimization of "How" without respect for "Why".
4. **Historical Discontinuity**: Loss of causal lineage back to Phase 1.
