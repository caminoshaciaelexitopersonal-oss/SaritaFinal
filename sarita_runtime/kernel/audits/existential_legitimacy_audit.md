# Existential Legitimacy Audit - SARITA Phase 98

## Component Analysis

### ConstitutionalIdentityEngine (Phase 97)
- **Findings**: Preserves the "What" (Identity), but not the "Why" (Legitimacy).
- **Gaps**: An identity can be preserved even if it no longer serves a constitutional purpose or benefit.

### OntologicalContinuityEngine (Phase 97)
- **Findings**: Proves entity persistence.
- **Gaps**: Continuity is justified by the past (being the same entity), not by the future benefit of existence.

### EssencePreservationEngine (Phase 97)
- **Findings**: Locks core invariants.
- **Gaps**: Invariants are protected regardless of their current legitimacy or necessity.

### Supreme Ontological Court (Phase 97)
- **Findings**: Adjudicates identity protection.
- **Gaps**: Does not judge if the existence of the entity itself is still justified.

## Detected Gaps
1. **Justification Deficit**: No measure of "Constitutional Benefit vs. Cost".
2. **Necessity Gap**: No analysis of "Constitutional Absence" (what if SARITA didn't exist?).
3. **Pure Survival Drift**: Survival and identity are prioritized even if they become a net cost to the constitutional substrate.
4. **Legitimacy Forgery**: Potential for the system to justify its own existence based on circular reasoning (surviving to survive).
