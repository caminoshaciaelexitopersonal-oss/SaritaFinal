# Predictive Governance Audit - Phase 108

## 1. Introduction
This audit assesses the readiness of SARITA for universal predictive governance.

## 2. Identified Brechas (PR-1 to PR-5)

### PR-1: Absence of Constitutional Prediction
- **Status**: IDENTIFIED
- **Description**: Currently, SARITA can discover and verify laws but cannot predict the next state of a constitution based on evolutionary pressures.
- **Remedy**: Implement `constitutional_prediction_engine.py`.

### PR-2: Absence of Civilizational Prediction
- **Status**: IDENTIFIED
- **Description**: Lack of long-horizon forecasting (1000+ generations) for civilizational success or failure.
- **Remedy**: Implement `civilizational_forecasting_engine.py`.

### PR-3: Absence of Collapse Prediction
- **Status**: IDENTIFIED
- **Description**: No specific mechanism to detect systemic fragility and institutional collapse triggers.
- **Remedy**: Implement `collapse_prediction_engine.py`.

### PR-4: Absence of Multiversal Prediction
- **Status**: IDENTIFIED
- **Description**: Projections are currently limited to single-universe trajectories.
- **Remedy**: Implement `multiverse_forecasting_engine.py`.

### PR-5: Absence of Certified Probabilistic Prediction
- **Status**: IDENTIFIED
- **Description**: Predictions lack a formal certification of their probabilistic accuracy.
- **Remedy**: Implement `prediction_accuracy_engine.py`.

## 3. Audit Conclusion
Phase 108 is required to transition from a scientific observer to a predictive architect.
