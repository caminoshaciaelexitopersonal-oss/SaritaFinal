# Constitutional Multiverse Report

## Simulation Capabilities Analysis

### Current Limit
- **Single Universe**: The `AdaptiveEnvironmentEngine` successfully creates a sequence of stressors.
- **Throughput**: Approximately 5,000 events/sec (Single writer).
- **History**: Linear ledger storage.

### Phase 105 Multiverse Targets
- **Parallel Universes**: 10,000+
- **Total Generations**: 5,000 per universe.
- **Data Volume**: Massive expansion of metadata for civilizational history.

### Constraints & Risks
- **MCE-4 (Universe Contamination)**: Causal paths must be strictly isolated between universes.
- **MCE-4 (Computational Exhaustion)**: 10,000 universes require heavy optimization of the simulation loop (Transition to purely mathematical state-transition models rather than full vertex processing for every generation).
- **Result Aggregation**: Need a `MultiverseOutcomeAnalyzer` to find patterns in civilizational success across divergent initial conditions.
