# Supremacy Feasibility Assessment

## Strategic Feasibility (MCE-5)

### Mathematical Foundation
The transition from local fitness to global supremacy is mathematically feasible by defining the **Global Civilizational Supremacy Index (GCSI)** as a multi-objective optimization problem on the Pareto frontier of:
1. **Long-Term Survival (P_s)**
2. **Structural Integrity (I_c)**
3. **Evolutionary Capacity (E_c)**
4. **Legitimacy Density (L_d)**

### Implementation Roadmap
1. **Tournament Engine**: Essential for rank-based selection of dominant meta-constitutions.
2. **Supremacy Theorem Engine**: Required to move beyond "best guess" to "mathematical proof" of supremacy.
3. **Attack Resistance**: Supremacy must be demonstrated under active civilizational attacks (Phase 105.8).

### Risks
- **Supremacy Stagnation**: A meta-constitution might look supreme because it hasn't faced a specific "black swan" universe.
- **Complexity Explosion**: Meta-meta-evolutions might emerge if not capped by Axiomatic Governance.
