# Constitutional Discovery Audit (Phase 106.1)

## Overview
This audit evaluates SARITA's capacity for innovation beyond existing constitutional paradigms. While Phase 105 established civilizational supremacy, Phase 106 focuses on the *discovery* of entirely new axioms, governance structures, and civilizational designs.

## Analyzed Components

### 1. ConstitutionalGenome
- **Status**: Stable for representing known genes.
- **Limitation**: Structured for pre-defined gene types. Does not support emergent, dynamically discovered genomic structures (CD-3).

### 2. MetaConstitutionEngine
- **Status**: Generates archetypes from a known design space.
- **Limitation**: The design space itself is fixed. It cannot discover dimensions outside of its pre-mapped coordinates (CD-2).

### 3. CivilizationalSimulationEngine
- **Status**: Proves dominance of known models.
- **Limitation**: Does not reward or detect "novelty" as a primary driver of evolution (CD-4).

### 4. ConstitutionalAxiomRegistry (Phase 101)
- **Status**: Holds core axioms (Identity, Purpose, etc.).
- **Limitation**: Lacks an engine for the autonomous synthesis of *unprecedented* axioms (CD-5).

## Gap Identification

| Gap ID | Description | Severity |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| **CD-1** | **Paradigm Dependency**: System is anchored in traditional governance models (Monolith, Swarm, etc.). | Critical |
| **CD-2** | **Limited Search Space**: Search algorithms optimize within known boundaries rather than expanding the boundaries. | Critical |
| **CD-3** | **Absence of Emergence**: The system cannot recognize or stabilize emergent properties that do not fit the current schema. | High |
| **CD-4** | **Lack of Autonomous Innovation**: No mechanism for "creativity" or divergent architectural exploration. | High |
| **CD-5** | **Axiomatic Stagnation**: Axioms are treated as static foundations rather than evolveable or discoverable entities. | Critical |

## Conclusion
SARITA is currently an "Optimizer." Phase 106 will transform it into an "Inventor" by implementing the Discovery and Creativity engines.
