# Universal Governance Audit (Phase 107.1)

## Overview
This audit evaluates SARITA's capacity to move from constitutional invention to the discovery of universal laws of governance. While Phase 106 enabled autonomous invention, Phase 107 seeks to extract the underlying "Science of Governance" from the vast data generated by millions of simulations and discoveries.

## Analyzed Components

### 1. ConstitutionalDiscoveryEngine (Phase 106)
- **Status**: Efficiently discovers novel configurations.
- **Limitation**: Lacks an abstraction layer to extract *general principles* from successful discoveries.

### 2. CivilizationalInventionEngine (Phase 106)
- **Status**: Generates 1,000,000+ designs.
- **Limitation**: Does not perform cross-design comparative analysis to identify recurring success factors (UL-2).

### 3. ConstitutionalMultiverseEngine (Phase 105)
- **Status**: Simulates 10,000+ universes.
- **Limitation**: Provides raw outcome data but does not perform causal inference to separate correlation from true governance laws (UL-3, UL-4).

### 4. CivilizationalSupremacyEngine (Phase 105)
- **Status**: Certifies local/global dominance.
- **Limitation**: Certification is specific to a model, not a universal law applicable across models.

## Gap Identification

| Gap ID | Description | Severity |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| **UL-1** | **No Automatic Law Extraction**: The system sees successful configurations but doesn't "know" why they work in a generalized form. | Critical |
| **UL-2** | **No Detection of Universal Invariants**: Inability to identify principles that remain true across all 10,000+ universes. | Critical |
| **UL-3** | **Absence of Mathematical Causality**: Reliance on fitness scores rather than formal causal proofs of governance outcomes. | High |
| **UL-4** | **Correlation vs. Causality**: Risk of misidentifying accidental traits of successful civilizations as universal laws. | High |
| **UL-5** | **No Certification of Universal Laws**: Lack of a framework to formally certify a principle as a "Universal Law of Governance." | Critical |

## Conclusion
SARITA has the data (1,000,000+ designs, 10,000+ universes). It now needs the **Universal Governance Engine** to transform this data into formal science.
