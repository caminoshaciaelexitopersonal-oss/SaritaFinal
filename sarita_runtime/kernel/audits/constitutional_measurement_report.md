# Constitutional Measurement Report

## Metric Status
Currently, SARITA uses several "Scores" that lack formal grounding:

1. **Legitimacy Score**: Calculated in `existential_legitimacy_engine.py`. While numeric, it is not tied to a proof of constitutional survival.
2. **Strategic Value**: Calculated in `constitutional_value_engine.py`. Relies on a `utility` factor that is often estimated rather than proven.
3. **Alignment**: Purely boolean in most engines.

## Proposed Measurements (Phase 100.2)
- **Governance Quality Index (GQI)**: Measuring the stability of the decision-making path.
- **Constitutional Fitness Index (CFI)**: Measuring the system's aptitude for its current purpose.
- **Resilience Coefficient**: Measuring the ability to maintain invariants under attack.
- **Legitimacy Proof Density**: Ratio of proven decisions to total decisions.
