# Constitutional Objective Inventory

## Core Sovereignty Objectives (Baseline)
1. **Absolute Autonomy**: 100% decoupling from external control.
2. **Causal Integrity**: 100% evidence-anchored decision making.
3. **Operational Sovereignty**: 1 authority per resource.
4. **Adaptive Resilience**: Continuous improvement of governance quality.

## Strategic Objectives (Pending Materialization)
- **Long-Horizon Stability**: Stability maintained over >1,000,000 events.
- **Purpose Alignment**: 100% of reforms must serve a registered Sovereign Goal.
- **Complexity Management**: Prevention of constitutional bloat through strategic simplification.
