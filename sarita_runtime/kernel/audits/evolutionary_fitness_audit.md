# Evolutionary Fitness Audit - SARITA Phase 96

## Component Analysis

### ConstitutionalValueEngine (Phase 95)
- **Findings**: Measures goal utility, but utility doesn't necessarily equal *survival probability*.
- **Gaps**: A valuable goal might be too risky for long-term survival.

### ConstitutionalEconomyEngine (Phase 95)
- **Findings**: Manages evolutionary budgets.
- **Gaps**: Budget allocation is based on priority, not on *evolutionary advantage* or extinction risk mitigation.

### FutureCompetitionEngine (Phase 95)
- **Findings**: Selects dominant futures.
- **Gaps**: Selection criteria are value and stability. Missing explicit mathematical modeling of survival probability under extreme failure chains.

### ConstitutionalSurvivalEngine (Phase 95)
- **Findings**: Detects existential risks.
- **Gaps**: Reactive risk management. Needs to evolve into proactive *evolutionary selection* of high-fitness trajectories.

## Detected Gaps
1. **Selection Gap**: Decisions are made based on value, but not on survival probability.
2. **Fitness Deficit**: No measure of "Constitutional Fitness" (aptitude to persist).
3. **Extinction Blindness**: No formal modeling of catastrophic failure chains or extinction probability.
4. **Reserve Deficit**: No dedicated "Survival Reserve" of resources for existential continuity.
