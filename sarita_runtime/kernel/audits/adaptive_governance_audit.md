# Adaptive Governance Audit - Phase 110

## 1. Introduction
This audit evaluates SARITA's capacity for autonomous adaptive governance, focusing on its ability to correct future trajectories in real-time.

## 2. Identified Brechas (AG-1 to AG-5)

### AG-1: Strategic Rigidity
- **Status**: IDENTIFIED
- **Description**: Current strategies are static once prescribed, lacking real-time reconfiguration.
- **Remedy**: Implement `adaptive_strategy_engine.py`.

### AG-2: Prescription Obsolescence
- **Status**: IDENTIFIED
- **Description**: Prescriptions do not account for environmental decay or context shifts over time.
- **Remedy**: Implement `obsolescence_detection_engine.py`.

### AG-3: Environmental Drift
- **Status**: IDENTIFIED
- **Description**: Changes in economic, cultural, or technological contexts are not currently detected as governance stressors.
- **Remedy**: Implement `environment_change_engine.py`.

### AG-4: Temporal Misalignment
- **Status**: IDENTIFIED
- **Description**: Decisions made at t0 may become misaligned by t50 due to evolutionary branching.
- **Remedy**: Implement `continuous_governance_engine.py`.

### AG-5: Delayed Adaptation
- **Status**: IDENTIFIED
- **Description**: The feedback loop between observation and correction has significant latency.
- **Remedy**: Implement `real_time_feedback_engine.py`.

## 3. Audit Conclusion
SARITA must evolve into an Autonomous Adaptive Governor to maintain constitutional relevance in a changing universe.
