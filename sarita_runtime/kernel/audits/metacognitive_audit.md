# Metacognitive Audit - SARITA Phase 93

## Component Analysis

### ConstitutionalLearningEngine
- **Status**: Operational.
- **Findings**: Learns patterns from history, but lacks a feedback loop to verify if the learned patterns actually lead to better governance.
- **Gaps**: No self-evaluation of learning quality.

### ConstitutionalSimulationEngine
- **Status**: Operational.
- **Findings**: Projects scenarios and predicts outcomes.
- **Gaps**: Simulations are never compared with real outcomes after a reform is applied. No accuracy tracking.

### ConstitutionalReformEngine
- **Status**: Operational.
- **Findings**: Manages the lifecycle of reforms.
- **Gaps**: Reforms are applied if they pass initial simulation and court review, but their long-term success is not measured or used to improve future reform generation.

### ConstitutionalReformCourt
- **Status**: Operational.
- **Findings**: Reviews legality and technical compliance.
- **Gaps**: Does not judge the "quality" or "wisdom" of a reform, only its adherence to current rules.

## Detected Gaps
1. **Incomplete Learning Cycles**: Learning occurs, but its effectiveness is not monitored.
2. **Simulation without Feedback**: Prediction accuracy is not tracked.
3. **Reform without Result Measurement**: No "success" metric for applied reforms.
4. **Absence of Evolutionary Metrics**: The system doesn't know if it's getting "better" at governing itself.
