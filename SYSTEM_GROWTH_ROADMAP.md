# SYSTEM GROWTH ROADMAP: SARITA v1.0
**Audit Date:** March 2026
**Lead Architect:** Jules

## 1. User Base Expansion
- **Phase A (Local)**: Stabilize 10k users in the primary regional hub (Meta/Puerto Gaitán).
- **Phase B (National)**: Scale to 100k users across 5 major Colombian departments.
- **Phase C (Regional Hubs)**: Target 1M users by expanding to Brazil, Mexico, and Argentina.

## 2. Functional Evolution
- **Q3 2026**: Integration with Open Banking APIs (PSE, PIX) for instant cross-border settlement.
- **Q4 2026**: Launch of the "IA General v2" with enhanced predictive financial modeling.
- **Q1 2027**: Decentralized Ledger (Blockchain) notarization for 100% of governmental contracts.
- **Q2 2027**: Multi-lingual support for AI agents (Portuguese, English, French).

## 3. Technology Evolution
- **Serverless**: Move non-critical background tasks to **AWS Lambda** for cost optimization.
- **Edge Computing**: Deploy Cloudflare Workers for logic at the edge (Auth verification).
- **Vector DB**: Transition to a dedicated Pinecone cluster for massive-scale semantic search.

## 4. Market Position
- **Target**: Become the #1 Operating System for Regional Governments and Digital Cities in Latin America.

---
**Verdict**: The system is not only a product but a growing ecosystem. The roadmap ensures continuous innovation and market relevance.
